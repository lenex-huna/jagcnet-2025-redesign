16th Legal Operations Detachment

AREA OF RESPONSIBILITY

The 16th Legal Operations Detachment (LOD) provides defense counsel services primarily to United States Army Reserve Soldiers where the Separation Authority, unit, or Soldier is located within the Northeastern half of the United States. These include Minnesota, Wisconsin, Illinois, Michigan, Indiana, and all states northeast beginning at Kentucky and Delaware and ending at Maine. The 16th LOD territory also includes Germany.
