TJAG & CWOC Sends Vol. 42-06 - Recipient of the Inaugural Chief Warrant Officer Three (CW3) Melanie D. Sellars Servant Leadership Award

Posted: 11/14/2024 02:09:20 PM EST

Event Date(s): From: Nov 15, 2024 To: Nov 15, 2024

Recipient of the Inaugural Chief Warrant Officer Three (CW3) Melanie D. Sellars Servant Leadership Award

We are excited to recognize the recipient of the inaugural CW3 Melanie D. Sellars Servant Leadership Award, Chief Warrant Officer Two (CW2) Francisco (Frank) A. Castellanos.

This award honors the memory and selfless service of CW3 Sellars, a superb Legal Administrator, who embodied two vital aspects of the four constants-stewardship and servant leadership. Throughout her career, CW3 Sellars built meaningful relationships with her teammates and created a culture where everyone could thrive. Her unparalleled positivity, relentless problem-solving ability, and can-do attitude improved every organization in which she served. CW3 Sellars was also an active volunteer, working with Habitat for Humanity, coaching youth sports, and building playgrounds in underprivileged communities. She was extremely proud to be a Warrant Officer in our Corps. The establishment of this award ensures her lasting and significant positive mark on our Corps is remembered. Her legacy lives on through our teammates who continue to carry on her legacy of excellence every day around our Corps.

The recipient of this award, CW2 Castellanos, truly embodies the Servant Leadership spirit of CW3 Sellars. Just like CW3 Sellars, he epitomizes what it means to be a team player, fostering a caring, positive environment. He has a proven track record of stewarding the profession, refining and improving processes in every office he administers, and analyzing and refining systems and tools to leave each place better than he found it. Like CW3 Sellars before him, CW2 Castellanos is an active volunteer in his community-even training his dog Skye to become a service dog to uplift veterans in hospice care in his local communities.

Congratulations to CW2 Castellanos. We appreciate everything he does for our Corps, and no doubt, will continue to do. Thanks to our entire Warrant Officer community-you truly keep our Corps running. We hope the exemplary service of CW3 Sellars continues to inspire all of us to live up to her legacy.
