22nd Legal Operations Detachment

AREA OF RESPONSIBILITY

The 22d Legal Operations Detachment, provides defense counsel services primarily to United States Army Reserve Soldiers where the Separation Authority, unit, or Soldier is located within the Western half of the United States. These include all States west of the Mississippi River Except: Minnesota, Iowa, Missouri, Arkansas and Louisiana.

SOLDIER RESOURCES

Soldiers expecting UCMJ action and/or administrative separation: Click here to download PDF, read instructions, complete form, and submit per instructions. An Army Reserve TDS Attorney will contact you within 7 days.

For more information about TDS, please visit the following link: https://www.usar.army.mil/Commands/Functional/Legal-Command/Legal-Resources/Trial-Defense/
