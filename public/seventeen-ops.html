154th Legal Operations Detachment

AREA OF RESPONSIBILITY

The 154th Legal Operations Detachment (LOD) provides defense counsel services primarily to United States Army Reserve Soldiers where the Separation Authority, unit, or Soldier is located within the Southeastern United States. These include Virginia, North Carolina, South Carolina, Georgia, Florida, Alabama, Tennessee, Mississippi, Missouri, Louisiana, Arkansas and Puerto Rico.

COMMAND

COL George Boguslawski is the Commander of the 154th Legal Operations Detachment. LTC Clay West is the Deputy Commander. The Legal Administrator is CW2 Arndrell Waters, and the Chief Paralegal NCO is MSG Martha D. Olivarez.
