Notice of Passing – Colonel (Retired) Bryan H. Schempf

It is with deep sadness that we inform you of the passing of Colonel (Retired) Bryan H. Schempf of Williamsburg, Virginia, who passed away on 14 October 2023. He was 76 years old.

Bryan Heaton Schempf was born on 6 April 1947 in Rochester, New York, to Marjorie and William Schempf. He graduated from the United States Military Academy in 1969 with a Bachelor of Science and obtained his Juris Doctor from the University of Missouri – Kansas City School of Law in 1974.

COL Schempf commissioned in the United States Army in 1969 prior to joining the JAG Corps in 1974. His military assignments included Staff Judge Advocate, United States Army Training and Doctrine Command, Fort Monroe, VA; United States Army Recruiting Command, Fort Sheridan, IL; United States Army Claims Service – East, APO, NY; United States Army Military Personnel Center, St. Louis, MO; Staff and Faculty at The Judge Advocate General’s School, Charlottesville, VA; and United States Army Garrison, Fort Meade, MD. He was a graduate of the 74th Judge Advocate Officer Basic Course, the 26th Judge Advocate Advanced Course, and the United States Army Command and Staff General College.

Bryan is survived by his wife, Marilyn; daughters Allison and Laura (son-in-law Marc); grandsons Marco and Theo; siblings Ruthanne (brother-in-law, Joel) and Kevin (sister-in-law Jo Ann); and brothers-in-law Allen Vandel (Shirley) and Jerry Vandel. A Celebration of Life was held at the Williamsburg United Methodist Church, 500 Jamestown Road, Williamsburg, Virginia on 28 October 2023. In lieu of flowers, the family requests donations be made in Bryan's name to the charity of your choice. Online condolences may be offered to the family at https://bucktroutfuneralhome.net/obituary/bryan-heaton-schempf/.

Please keep the Schempf family in your thoughts and prayers during this most difficult time.
