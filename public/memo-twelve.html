Notice of Passing – Captain Chad Ryan Christian

It is with deep sadness that we inform you of the passing of CPT Chad Ryan Christian, of Huntsville, Alabama. CPT Christian passed away on 25 March 2024. He was 37 years old.

Chad Ryan Christian was born on 26 January 1987 in Huntsville, Alabama, to Robert and Marsha Christian. He attended the University of Alabama in 2010, graduating with a Bachelor of Science Degree in Finance and Operations Research and a Bachelor of Science Degree in Applied Finance. He quickly realized his aspirations to serve his country and was awarded a full ROTC scholarship, commissioning as a second lieutenant into the United States Army. He was selected for the Funded Legal Education Program (FLEP) in 2015, attending Vanderbilt Law School in Nashville, Tennessee, where he graduated with his Juris Doctor Degree in 2018.

CPT Christian’s military assignments include Contract Law Attorney, Army Contracting Command, Redstone Arsenal, Alabama; Trial Counsel, 1st Infantry Division, Fort Riley, Kansas; Military Justice Attorney, 1st Infantry Division, Fort Riley, Kansas; Legal Assistance Attorney, 1st Infantry Division, Fort Riley, Kansas; Maneuver Branch Officer-in-Charge, 7th Army Training Command, Rose Barr, Germany; Executive Officer, 172nd Infantry Brigade, East Camp, Germany; and Platoon Leader, 2nd Battalion, 28th Infantry Regiment, Grafenwoehr, Germany.

CPT Christian’s military awards and decorations include the Bronze Star, Army Commendation Medal (5th Award), Army Achievement Medal (2nd Award), Meritorious Unit Citation, National Defense Service Medal, Global War on Terrorism Service Medal, Afghanistan Campaign Medal (two campaign stars), Humanitarian Service Medal, and Combat Action Badge. He was a graduate of the Officer Basic Course and the Air Assault Course.

He is survived by his wife, Rachel, his parents Robert and Marsha Christian, brother Alan (Kristin) Christian, brother Tanner (Claire) Christian, sister Amanda (John) Evans, sister Whitney (Huy) Huynh, nephews Matthew, Malachi and Henry, nieces Allie, Eleanor, Emily, Diana and Vivienne, his grandmother Judith Christian Moon, as well as many beloved aunts, uncles and cousins.

Funeral services for CPT Christian were held on 3 April 2024 at Berryhill Funeral Home, Huntsville, Alabama. A committal service was held on 4 April 2024 at the Alabama National Cemetery in Montevallo, Alabama, with full military honors. CPT Christian’s full obituary can be found at https://www.berryhillfh.com/obituary/chad-christian.

Please keep the Christian family in your thoughts and prayers during this most difficult of times.
