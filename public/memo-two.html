It is with deep sadness that we inform you of the passing of SFC (Ret.) Daniel J. Nededog of Fayetteville, North Carolina. SFC Nededog passed away on Saturday, August 10, 2024. He was 48 years old.

SFC (Ret.) Nededog was born on October 17, 1975 to Antonio C. Nededog and the late Delia S.N. Nededog. He enlisted in the US Army on September 18, 2008 and served as an infantry Soldier at Fort Richardson, Alaska. He later joined the Judge Advocate General’s Corps as a paralegal. SFC (Ret.) Nededog spent the majority of his career at Fort Bragg (now Fort Liberty), North Carolina. He served multiple times as a Senior Brigade Paralegal with the 82d Airborne Division.

SFC (Ret.) Nededog is survived by his wife, Zina; his daughter, Erin; his father & stepmother, Antonio C. and Ellen; his brothers, sisters, and their spouses: Anthony P. & Misty, Geraldine S.N. Serralta, Marie N. & Francisco Guerrero, Denise N. & Benjamin Eseroma, Andrew J. & Imee; and Sean C. & Kayla; his mother-in-law, Soledad “Laling” B. Duenas; his brothers-in-law and their spouses: Steven P. & Shannon Duenas, and Robert A. & Deborah Duenas; and many other nephews, nieces, cousins, friends, loved ones, and Soldiers.

The family is welcoming visitors to celebrate the life of SFC (Ret.) Nededog on Tuesday, August 20, 2024 in North Carolina. The viewing will take place from 10:00 to 11:15 a.m., followed by the rosary at 11:30, and a funeral mass from 12:00 to 1:15 p.m. at Pinecrest Funeral and Cremation Services of Hope Mills, North Carolina. Interment with full military honors will be at 2:00 p.m. at Sandhills State Veterans Cemetery in Spring Lake.

Please keep the Nededog family in your thoughts and prayers during this most difficult of times.
