Notice of Passing – Mrs. Kathryn "Kate" Leigh Richards

It is with deep sadness that we inform you of the passing of Mrs. Kathryn "Kate" Leigh Richards of Linden, North Carolina, who passed away on 25 May 2024. She was 45 years old.

Kathryn Leigh Richards was born on 22 July 1978 in Jonesboro, Georgia. Kate graduated with a Master’s Degree in Forensic Psychology from Argosy University.

Kate served as the Victim/Witness Liaison for I Corps, Joint Base Lewis-McChord, Washington, before being medically retired in April 2020. Prior to serving as the I Corps Victim/Witness Liaison, Kate worked for the United States Attorney’s Office in Flagstaff, Arizona, a Maximum Security Prison in South Carolina, and the United States Attorney’s Office in Alaska as a Victim Advocate. She dedicated her career to being a victim advocate, providing support and compassion to those in need.

Kate is survived by her husband, Norman (Norm) Richards, and their daughters, Addison Grace Richards and Sophia Ann Richards; her mother, Marilyn Manning Plunkett; her sister and brother-in-law, Allison and Lee Attaway, and their son, Kyle Attaway; her sister, Jessica Plunkett; her best friend, Shannon Johnson, and multiple aunts, uncles, and cousins.

A memorial service for Kate was held on 2 June 2024 at Rogers and Breece Chapel, Fayetteville, North Carolina. In lieu of flowers, the family kindly requests contributions to the account set up for their daughters – https://gofund.me/f3d77824.

Kate’s full obituary can be found at https://www.rogersandbreece.com/obituary/KathrynKate-Richards.

Please keep the Richards family in your thoughts and prayers during this difficult time.
