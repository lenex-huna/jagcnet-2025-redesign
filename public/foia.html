Freedom of Information Act (FOIA)

The Judge Advocate General is authorized to act on requests for records relating to claims, courts-martial, legal services, administrative investigations and similar legal records. Records will be made available consistent with applicable laws and regulations. Information will be withheld from the public only if authorized by 5 U.S.C. Section 552 (Freedom of Information Act (FOIA), 5 U.S.C. Section 552a (Privacy Act), or other statutory or regulatory authority.

The Office of The Judge Advocate General (OTJAG) is organized into two field operating agencies and a number of divisions. Within OTJAG, each component processes its own records. Therefore, your request will receive the quickest possible response if it is addressed directly to the component that you believe has the records you are seeking.
