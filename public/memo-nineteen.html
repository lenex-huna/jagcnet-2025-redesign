Notice of Passing – Colonel (Retired) Roger Wade Cornelius

It is with deep sadness that we inform you of the passing of Colonel (Retired) Roger Wade Cornelius of Huntsville, Alabama, who passed away on 29 November 2023. He was 73 years old.

Roger Wade Cornelius was born on 12 September 1950, in Wooster, Ohio, to Wade and Georgene Keim Cornelius. He graduated from Wooster High School in 1968, obtained his Bachelor of Science degree in History from Bowling Green State University in Ohio in 1972, and his Juris Doctor degree from Akron Law School in 1975.

COL (Ret) Cornelius commissioned in the U.S. Army JAG Corps in January 1976. His military assignments included Deputy Chief Counsel, US Army Aviation and Missile Command, Redstone Arsenal, AL; Chief Attorney, Washington, D.C.; Deputy Command Counsel, US Army Contracting Command Europe, Seckenheim, GE; Contract Legal Advisor, US Army Headquarters Command, Fort Monmouth, NJ; Senior Instructor for Contract Law, The Judge Advocate General’s School (TJAGSA) Charlottesville, VA; Instructor for Contract Law, TJAGSA, Charlottesville, VA; Assistant Judge Advocate, US Army Ballistic Missile Defense, Huntsville, AL; Claims Judge Advocate and Assistant Staff Judge Advocate, US Army Combined Arms Center, Fort Leavenworth, KS. He was a graduate of the Army War College, Command and General Staff College, Judge Advocate Officer Graduate Course, and Judge Advocate Officer Basic Course.

Roger retired from the U.S. Army in 2002 after 27 years in the JAG Corps. He started his second career as a Civilian Contract Law Attorney in the AMCOM Legal office at Redstone Arsenal, where he remained until his second retirement in 2020.

Roger is survived by his loving wife of 51 years, Debbie Cornelius of Huntsville; his brother, Jeff (Chris) Cornelius of Key Largo, FL; and his beloved nephews and niece.

A Celebration of Life Service was held on 8 December 2023, at Whitesburg Baptist Church South Campus in Huntsville, Alabama. COL (Ret) Cornelius will be buried in Arlington National Cemetery at a later date.
